Welcome to the Global Student Leadership Summit!

Join us for an inspiring journey towards nurturing tomorrow's global leaders.

GSLS 2024 Timeline

  • July 31st 2024

    Registration and Full Payment

    Registration for GSLS 2024 closes on July 31st, 2024. To secure your spot, be sure to complete your registration and submit your full payment by this date.

  • August 15th 2024

    Deadline for Abstract Submission

    The deadline for submitting abstracts for GSLS 2024 is August 15th, 2024. We encourage you to submit your high-quality and in-depth abstracts by this date.

  • August 22nd 2024

    Deadline for Full Paper Submission

    The deadline for submitting full papers for GSLS 2024 is August 22nd, 2024. We encourage you to submit your well-developed and comprehensive research papers by this date.

  • August 30th 2024

    Slides Submission

    The deadline for submitting slides for your Presentation for GSLS 2024 is August 22nd, 2024. We encourage you to submit your Presentation Slides by this date.

  • September 9-10 2024

    Conference GSLS

    The prestigious GSLS Conference will be convening on September 9th and 10th, 2024, at the esteemed Istanbul Universitesi. This two-day event promises to be a stimulating forum for scholars and researchers to engage in thought-provoking discussions and presentations centered around.
